\begin{abstract}
	The Cartan formula relates the cup product and the action of the Steenrod algebra on mod~$p$ cohomology.
	For any pair of mod~$p$ cocycles in a simplicial set, where $p$ is an odd prime, we explicitly construct a natural coboundary descending to this relation in cohomology.
\end{abstract}
